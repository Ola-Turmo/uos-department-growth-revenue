# Research Brief: Funnel Diagnosis Framework

**Date**: 2026-04-13  
**Confidence Level**: Medium  
**Author**: Growth Revenue Department  

## Topic

Framework for diagnosing funnel conversion issues

## Key Findings

1. **Stage-level analysis** identifies specific bottlenecks
   - Calculate conversion rate per stage
   - Identify stages with >30% drop-off as priority

2. **Drop-off reason inference** uses behavioral signals
   - Exit pages indicate friction points
   - Time in stage reveals engagement issues

3. **Benchmark comparison** contextualizes performance
   - Industry benchmarks vary by stage
   - Consider product complexity and price point

## Funnel Stage Benchmarks

| Stage | Good | Average | Poor |
|-------|------|---------|------|
| Awareness → Interest | >40% | 25-40% | <25% |
| Interest → Consideration | >30% | 15-30% | <15% |
| Consideration → Intent | >25% | 10-25% | <10% |
| Intent → Purchase | >15% | 5-15% | <5% |

## Diagnosis Workflow

1. Calculate stage-by-stage conversion rates
2. Identify worst-performing stage
3. Analyze drop-off reasons (exit pages, time in stage)
4. Compare to benchmarks
5. Generate hypothesis for improvement

## Follow-up Questions

1. What are our actual benchmarks based on historical data?
2. How do we handle multi-channel entry points?
3. When should we recommend full funnel vs. stage-specific optimization?
