# Research Brief: Enterprise Segment Analysis

**Date**: 2026-04-13  
**Confidence Level**: Medium-High  
**Author**: Growth Revenue Department  

## Topic

Enterprise customer behavior patterns and conversion characteristics

## Key Findings

1. **Longer consideration cycles** for enterprise deals
   - Average 3-6 months from first touch to conversion
   - Multiple stakeholders involved in decisions

2. **Channel preferences** differ from SMB
   - Heavy reliance on direct sales and demos
   - Content downloads less predictive of conversion

3. **Lead scoring** must account for:
   - Company size indicators
   - Content engagement patterns (whitepapers, case studies)
   - Demo/trial usage

## Enterprise vs. SMB Comparison

| Factor | Enterprise | SMB |
|--------|------------|-----|
| Avg. Consideration Period | 3-6 months | 2-4 weeks |
| Key Content Types | Whitepapers, Case Studies | Blog Posts, Webinars |
| Demo Importance | Very High | Medium |
| Price Sensitivity | Medium | High |
| Decision Makers | Multiple | Single |

## Segmentation Criteria

Enterprise classification based on:
- Company size (>500 employees)
- Industry vertical
- Annual revenue indicators
- Website engagement depth
