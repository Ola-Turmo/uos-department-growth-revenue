# Funnel Diagnosis Playbook

## Purpose
Systematic approach to diagnosing funnel conversion issues

## Trigger Conditions
- Overall conversion rate below target
- Specific stage with high drop-off
- Revenue below expectations

## Step-by-Step Procedure

### 1. Gather Funnel Data
1. Collect stage-by-stage metrics
2. Identify total users at each stage
3. Note drop-off points

### 2. Calculate Key Metrics
- Stage conversion rates
- Drop-off rates per stage
- Time spent per stage
- Revenue at each stage

### 3. Identify Bottlenecks
- Stage with lowest conversion rate
- Stage with highest drop-off
- Stage with largest revenue impact

### 4. Analyze Drop-off Reasons
- Exit page analysis
- Time in stage distribution
- User segment breakdown

### 5. Generate Hypothesis
- What causes the bottleneck?
- What changes could improve conversion?
- Priority based on impact vs. effort

### 6. Recommend Actions
1. High-impact, low-effort: Quick win
2. High-impact, high-effort: Major initiative
3. Low-impact: Deprioritize

## Success Criteria
- Bottleneck identified with confidence
- Hypothesis generated for improvement
- Recommendations prioritized

## Escalation Points
- No clear bottleneck → Request more data
- External factors suspected → Escalate to leadership
- Conflicting signals → Deeper segment analysis
